A project for Nottingham Food Bank

Given by Postlight

Complete! Shipped on December 12th, 2018. View case study

The Problem We have an on-site form for donators, and that data is manually input into salesforce for future contacts. This takes up an immense amount of time and is very error-prone.

Statement of Work A solution that will tie the on-site form to Salesforce automatically, creating one single database as a source of truth for your donators

Technical Documentation Sets up a callback hook to update the records and sends the data to salesforce.
